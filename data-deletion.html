Last Updated: November 1, 2024

Your Right to Data Deletion

At Agents2Automate, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of your data at any time.

When you use our WhatsApp automation services, we may collect:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • WhatsApp conversation data (if you use our services)
  • Business information (company name, industry)
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers)

Data Deletion Process

  1. Request Received: We acknowledge your request within 24 hours
  2. Identity Verification: We verify your identity to protect your data (1-2 business days)
  3. Data Review: We identify all data associated with your account
  4. Deletion: We permanently delete your data from our systems (within 30 days)
  5. Confirmation: You receive confirmation once deletion is complete

Timeline

We will complete your data deletion request within 30 days of verification. In most cases, deletion is completed within 7-14 days.
